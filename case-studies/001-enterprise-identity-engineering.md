# Case Study 001

# Engineering Enterprise Identity Governance

---

## Problem

Enterprise identity information was fragmented across multiple systems.

Role assignments, privileged accounts, service accounts, authentication, ownership, and operational visibility were difficult to correlate.

Identity administration relied heavily on manual effort and organizational knowledge.

This increased operational overhead while reducing confidence in engineering decisions.

---

## Engineering Objective

Design repeatable identity engineering practices that improve visibility, reduce manual administration, standardize governance, and provide stronger foundations for security operations.

---

## Constraints

Highly regulated environment

Cloud infrastructure

Hybrid identity

Operational continuity requirements

CMMC readiness

Limited engineering resources

---

## Engineering Decisions

Standardize Microsoft Entra governance.

Engineer repeatable RBAC processes.

Develop service account lifecycle management.

Improve privileged identity visibility.

Leverage Microsoft Graph for automation.

Document engineering standards.

Reduce manual administrative effort.

---

## Technologies

Microsoft Entra ID

Microsoft Graph

Azure

PowerShell

Python

REST APIs

RBAC

Conditional Access

---

## Results

Improved engineering consistency.

Reduced operational overhead.

Increased visibility into privileged identities.

Created repeatable governance processes.

Established engineering standards supporting long-term maintainability.

Provided architectural foundation supporting future identity correlation within USOP.

---

## Lessons Learned

Identity is not simply authentication.

Identity becomes the contextual layer connecting every security decision.

When identity engineering improves, cloud security, vulnerability management, detection engineering, and incident response all improve with it.

---

## Future Direction

Expand identity correlation beyond directory services.

Integrate infrastructure.

Integrate vulnerabilities.

Integrate attack paths.

Integrate threat intelligence.

Provide AI-assisted identity decision support through USOP.
