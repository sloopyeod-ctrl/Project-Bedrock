Security teams already have more data than they can process.

More dashboards.

More alerts.

More notifications.

More reports.

More tools.

The industry does not have an information problem.

It has a decision problem.

Security engineers spend too much of their time gathering information before they can begin solving security problems.

USOP exists to change that.

Every engineering decision inside USOP is measured against one question.

Does this improve the quality and speed of security decisions?

If not...

It does not belong in the platform.

The objective is simple.

Give security engineers their time back.

Everything else is implementation.
