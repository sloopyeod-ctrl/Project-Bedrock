# Why I Lead

Leadership has never been about authority.

It has always been about responsibility.

Throughout my military career and my engineering career I learned that systems become stronger when people have the tools, knowledge, and confidence to solve problems independently.

I do not believe leaders exist to become bottlenecks.

I believe leaders exist to remove them.

That belief influences every engineering decision I make.

Documentation exists because future engineers deserve a better starting point.

Automation exists because engineers should spend more time solving meaningful problems.

Standards exist because consistency improves quality.

Knowledge should never disappear because one person leaves an organization.

The greatest compliment an engineer can receive is not recognition.

It is seeing others build successfully upon the foundation they created.

That is the type of leader I continue striving to become.
