# Project Bedrock
## Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Version: 1.0
Status: Active
Owner: Marvin G. Dewitt

---

# Purpose

Project Bedrock exists to become the definitive engineering knowledge base documenting the professional evolution, engineering philosophy, technical accomplishments, systems, and lessons learned throughout my career.

Rather than treating resumes, portfolios, interviews, articles, and professional branding as separate efforts, Bedrock establishes a single engineering source of truth from which every professional artifact is generated.

---

# Mission

Engineer systems that enable security professionals to spend more time protecting infrastructure and less time managing technology.

---

# Vision

Create the most complete engineering knowledge base of my professional career.

The objective is not simply to obtain employment.

The objective is to continuously document engineering growth, preserve technical knowledge, demonstrate engineering judgment, and leave behind resources that help future engineers build stronger systems.

---

# Problem Statement

Most professional accomplishments disappear.

Projects end.

Companies change.

Ticketing systems are archived.

Documentation becomes inaccessible.

Lessons learned are forgotten.

Resumes capture only a fraction of an engineer's actual contributions.

Project Bedrock solves this problem by continuously documenting engineering knowledge while preserving both technical accomplishments and engineering philosophy.

---

# Success Criteria

Project Bedrock succeeds when:

• Every meaningful engineering accomplishment is documented.
• Future resumes are generated instead of rewritten.
• Recruiters understand my experience.
• Engineers understand my thinking.
• Future teammates understand my philosophy.
• My children can one day understand the work I dedicated my career to building.
